VoxStocks Real Time Stock Analysis and Monitoring

VoxStocks is a real-time stock analysis and monitoring pipeline built to process live trade data as it flows through the system. The architecture ingests trade events through an API, processes and routes them with Kafka and Spark, and stores data in PostgreSQL.

The pipeline is also continuously monitored with Prometheus and Grafana to detect unexpected shutdowns and ensure trade flow remains stable, reliable, and observable.
